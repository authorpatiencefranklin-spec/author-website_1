Easy Bake

Biscuit B*tch Cookie

A crunchy-chewy cookie combining beloved classics into one sweet, satisfying bake — perfect with coffee, tea, or a quiet reading break.

Prep · 20 min Bake · 10–12 min Yield · 9 cookies Difficulty · Easy
Ingredients Instructions Watch video
What you need

Ingredients

  • 115 g brown sugar
  • 150 g white sugar
  • 115 g butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 8 shortbread cookies, crushed (about 1 cup)
  • 10 Oreo cookies, crushed (about 1 cup)
  • 100 g chocolate chips, or as much as you like

For a lighter vegan version, swap the egg for a flaxseed or chia mixture and use plant-based butter.

Step by step

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter gently and let it cool slightly for about 10 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugars and butter until smooth.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Fold in the crushed cookies and chocolate chips.
  7. Preheat the oven to 170°C / 350°F.
  8. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto an ungreased baking sheet.
  9. Press the dough balls down slightly for a more even shape and softer texture.
  10. Bake for 10–12 minutes, until lightly golden.
  11. Let the cookies cool on the tray for a few minutes before moving them to a wire rack.
A little note

Dedication

This cookie is dedicated to every cool Biscuit B*tch out there who loves the ultimate crunch. May it pair beautifully with your favorite mug of tea or coffee — and satisfy every cookie craving.
