About Patience Franklin

Author of 'Fictional Messy Reality'.

Patience Franklin is a writer and author. With a passion for storytelling, she has crafted a unique voice that resonates with readers around the world. Her debut novel, Access, explores themes of love, loss, and self-discovery, earning critical acclaim and a dedicated fanbase.

In addition to her writing career, Patience is an avid baker and often shares her favorite cookie recipes on her website. When she's not writing or baking, she enjoys spending time with her family and friends, exploring the outdoors and taking up a variety of hobbies..

Follow Patience on all her official social media platforms to stay updated on her latest projects and cookie creations!
