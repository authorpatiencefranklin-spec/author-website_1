About Patience Franklin

Author of 'Fictional Messy Reality'.

Patience Franklin is a writer and author. With a passion for storytelling and sport, she is on her way to developing a unique voice that resonates with fans and readers around the world. Her debut novel, Access, explores themes of love, loss, and self-discovery, whilst in the dynamic environment surrounded by vicious blood, satisfying sweat and painful tears. earning critical acclaim and a dedicated fanbase.

In addition to her growing writing career, Patience is a passionate hobby-baker and often shares her favorite cookie and dessert recipes on her website. When she's not writing, baking or watching sweaty hotties perform exhausting sports, she enjoys spending time with her family and friends, exploring the outdoors and taking up a variety of crafty hobbies..

Follow Patience on all her official social media platforms to stay updated on her latest projects and cookie creations!
