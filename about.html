About the Author

Writing stories about love,
longing, and becoming

Bittersweet contemporary romance shaped by emotion, intimacy, and the quiet ache of self-discovery.

Hello, lovely reader

I’m Patience Franklin

I write contemporary romance with a bittersweet heart ♡ stories about desire, vulnerability, self-discovery, and the complicated ways we learn to love both others and ourselves.

My work is drawn to emotionally charged settings, aching tenderness, and characters who are trying to find their footing in the middle of ambition, loss, longing, and hope.

When I’m not writing, I’m usually baking, dreaming up new stories, watching sweaty, hot men perform sports, or sharing a few favorite cookie recipes here on the website. I also love quiet rituals, creative hobbies, and the kind of stories that stay with you long after the final page.
